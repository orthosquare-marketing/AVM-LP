Privacy Policy

At AVMSmiles, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, including all associated pages, subdomains, and related mobile applications.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and other contact details.
  • Patient Information: Medical history, dental records, treatment preferences, and appointment details.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, and transaction history.
  • Communication: Messages, feedback, inquiries, and communications sent through our website.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and profile information for registered users.

B. Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, and other browsing patterns.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to track website usage.
  • Location Information: General geographic location based on IP address.

C. Information from Third Parties

  • Business Partners: Information shared with us by third-party service providers or partners.
  • Social Media: Information from social media platforms if you link your account to our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our dental services.
  • To process appointments, payments, and transactions.
  • To communicate with you about appointments, services, and updates.
  • To send promotional materials and newsletters, with your consent.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve website functionality.
  • To detect, prevent, and address fraud and security issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulations.

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission.
  • Secure password policies and authentication mechanisms.
  • Regular security audits and updates.
  • Limited access to personal information by authorized personnel.
  • Compliance with applicable data protection regulations.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With vendors, consultants, and contractors who assist us in operating our website and providing services.
  • Medical Professionals: With healthcare providers involved in your treatment with appropriate consent.
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of merger, acquisition, or asset sale.
  • Aggregate Data: With third parties in aggregate or anonymized form that cannot identify you.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal requirements.
  • Marketing Communications: Opt out of receiving promotional emails or newsletters.
  • Cookies: Control cookie preferences through your browser settings.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience. These may include:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Long-term cookies that remain on your device.
  • Analytics Cookies: Used to understand how visitors use our website.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and promotions.

You can control cookies through your browser settings or opt out of certain tracking through various mechanisms.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites before providing your personal information.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information and terminate the child's use of our website.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. The updated version will be posted on our website with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the website following the posting of revised Privacy Policy means that you accept and agree to the changes.

What Are Dental Implants?

Dental Implants are titanium posts placed into the jawbone to replace missing teeth. These implants serve as a foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures, restoring natural tooth function and appearance. They offer a long-lasting solution that prevents bone loss, supports jaw structure, and provides a stable, comfortable alternative to traditional dentures or bridges.
